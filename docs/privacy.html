Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Study Buddy App is a local study tool for Windows. It lets users paste class notes and generate study materials such as flashcards, quiz questions, key terms, and study plans.

Information We Collect

Study Buddy App does not collect, sell, or share personal information.

Pasted Notes

Pasted notes stay on the user's device. Version 1 of Study Buddy App does not upload notes to a server, cloud service, or third-party AI service.

Accounts

Study Buddy App does not require users to create an account.

Internet Use

Version 1 is designed to work locally and does not depend on cloud processing for generated study material.

Children and Students

Study Buddy App is intended as a study aid. Generated study material can contain mistakes, so students should compare generated content with their own class materials.

Future Changes

If future versions add cloud features, accounts, file uploads, paid AI, or analytics, this privacy policy will be updated before those features are released.
