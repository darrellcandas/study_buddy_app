Study Buddy App

A Windows study tool that turns pasted class notes into flashcards, quiz questions, key terms, and a simple study plan.

Privacy Policy Support

About

Study Buddy App is designed for students who want a quick way to review their own notes. Version 1 works locally with typed or pasted notes.

  • Paste notes
  • Generate a local study set
  • Review flashcards, quizzes, key terms, and study order
  • Mark flashcard confidence as Weak, Okay, or Strong

Important Note

Generated study material can contain mistakes. Students should compare cards, quizzes, and study plans with their own class materials.
